The clock has hands, the hands move round and round.

The clock has numbers, always the same numbers.

The clock moves forward, does it restart, is it the same day when it is at 2?

The clock can be cheated, tricked, illusioned.

The clock is dumb, does not know the year, the birth of the sun.

The clock is just a clock.
