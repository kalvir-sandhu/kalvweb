Update May 2026: Most of these projects are not public now as I'm working to keep them private until I can test them with real people.

How does one protect their soul, their mind, their work in 2026. I have heard so many odd terms be used in the news of today. Vibe Coding, Aura farming and Rage Bait. All items that I feel I've been suffering from as I've been looking for my new position after a lot of trauma that has been sent my way for being innocent, good, caring and giving with all that I do. Not one job offer has ever materialized for the work I have done and experience that I have. As though people are laughing at me.

To deal with this I've built a couple of things.

1. A brain clock. A unique clock sequence that is generated with a SHA256 shape that is geometrically drawn to my eyes every 1 second. My first version used dots to code it to my brain using the learn pyschology I have read about over the last year or so.
It runs on my iPad, iPhone and I've made a desktop version of it that is transparent that can float on top of the windows such that I always see it as I work.
![/img/posts/2026/kalv-time.avif](/img/posts/2026/kalv-time.avif)

Update 23 Jan 2026: I've updated this and the Daltu graphic to use a private key to create a unique SHA 256bit sum for each representation to ensure that the generated graphic uses a private key stored in the secure conclave. Meaning that it has now become a lava lamp that is unique to the machine it's being ran on and the mind viewing it. Discrete but very hard to generate elsewhere on any other computer.

2. A new text editor that creates a unique symbolic visualization against the language of choice and typography for creativity, inspiration, innovation and thinking. I called it Daltu from Hebrew root meaning 'door', a prefix meaning "that/which/from". And can remember reading that the first wax boards from Greeks was called a Daltu. Loved that it meant 'door' to knowledge. I took inspiration from learning early scripture and symbols used from early people 1000s of years ago, greeks, aztecs and even cave paintings from 10k years ago.

3. BPM based light. A new candle for the mind. It generates a light sequence that flashes on an iPhone based on a BPM that I tap into the phone. I love that I can now use this to detect BPM's of tracks that I mix music to with my Roland S-1 and OP-1 Field.
