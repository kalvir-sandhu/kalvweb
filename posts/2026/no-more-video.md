I recorded another private audio vlog on Apple Voice Memos and found it really a great way to own my own voice whilst listening to a uniquely composed track of music using a custom piano sound sample and op-1 field cpu frequency that is unique to my bedroom.

As I did, I want to post my thoughts on the world today requiring less visuals. I feel we don't listen and observe what we see with our eyes as much in today's western society.

It's as though we need radio again to listen to the exact words, perhaps people don't even listen to the music lyrics themselves appreciating tracks of music that are poetic vs a rhythm of the track, the beat and repeating sequences of sounds.

Movies are all visually impressive, but the written content is never as good as it used to be, it's as though the lines are just repeats of what previous movies have said, like looped lines.

Social media is never about the words and content, it's just about the visuals, the worst is women. All wearing barely any clothing to present themselves and never their brains and what the content is meant to portray. Blogging doesn't exist, and people sharing their mind with words and deep thinking or even just highlighting their expertize in being able to share their knowledge in 5minutes with real depth and insight.

I'd ideally like in the future to build a new product that allows all people of the world to record radio again easily, simply and have the content reach all people of the world, using local translation libraries to record in english and have a spanish listener listen to it as they walk to school in their small village in Mexico vs a fancy coffee shop in London.
